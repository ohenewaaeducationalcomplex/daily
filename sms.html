Install on Phone

Follow these steps to install the School Management System as an app on your phone:

For iOS (Safari):

  1. Open Safari and navigate to this page
  2. Tap the Share button at the bottom of the screen
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Tap "Add" in the top-right corner
  5. The app will now appear on your home screen

For Android (Chrome):

  1. Open Chrome and navigate to this page
  2. Tap the three-dot menu in the top-right corner
  3. Tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Confirm by tapping "Add"
  5. The app will now appear on your home screen

Once installed, you can access the School Management System directly from your home screen like any other app.
